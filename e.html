Our objects can come to life with animation.
They can speak, go places or become fantasy characters.
If your object could talk, what would it say? Just think about it.
How would it move? Would it look the same, or would it grow eyes and hands?
Where would it go?

You can add eyes, hands, or other features to your object using materials like paper, clay, fabric, or any other medium you choose.

You can also create a background where your object might want to go, imagining a place or environment that reflects its story or journey.
